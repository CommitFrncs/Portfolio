About Me

A little bit about
who I am.

I'm a self-taught frontend developer who fell in love with the web and never looked back. I specialise in building clean, well-structured interfaces using vanilla HTML, CSS & JavaScript — no heavy framework needed when the fundamentals are solid.

Beyond the code, I run @commitfrncs — a multi-platform content brand across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X, Facebook, Pinterest, and Medium. I document UI tips, developer mindset, and the honest realities of building a freelance career from scratch.

When I'm not shipping something, you'll find me deep in motorsport aesthetics, obsessing over type pairings, or reverse-engineering a design I like.
